## 1. Overview
This project analyzes train ride data from the UK railway system to identify travel patterns, delays, ticket demand, and revenue trends.  
The goal is to deliver actionable insights and a visualization dashboard for decision-makers.

---

## 2. Stakeholders
| Stakeholder          | Role                                | Interest in Project                     |
|----------------------|------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Data Analysis Team    | Data preparation, analysis, models | High-quality analysis, automation      |
| Project Manager      | Oversight, timeline, delivery      | Clear progress tracking                |
| Business Analysts    | Translate findings to actions      | Usable insights for planning           |
| Railway Operators    | Operational improvements           | Reduced delays, efficiency             |
| Decision Makers      | Strategic planning                 | Data-driven decisions                  |

---

## 3. Risks & Mitigation
| Risk                             | Impact                    | Mitigation                              |
|----------------------------------|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Incomplete or inaccurate data    | High                      | Validate and clean data thoroughly      |
| Delayed deliverables             | Medium                    | Use agile sprints, weekly check-ins     |
| Poor dashboard adoption          | Medium                    | Get stakeholder feedback early          |
| Forecasting model inaccuracy     | Medium                    | Test multiple models, validate results  |
| Tool compatibility issues        | Low                       | Test environment setup early            |
